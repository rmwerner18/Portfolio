About Me

I live in Nashville, TN, in an apartment close to downtown. I moved to the city to be a part of the music scene, which at first was not thriving, due to it being the height of covid. Now that it's booming again, I'm working on starting a blues rock band. Meanwhile, I'm working a job in construction in order to pay my bills. However, while I love the physical exercise, construction is not the field I hope to pursue. I'm instead working on getting a job in what I hope will become my long-term career: software engineering.
